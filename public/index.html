JaTeX

Amongst other things, LaTeX can be used to create PDF files that contain math and equations. However, LaTeX isn't highly accessible for everyone. LaTeX provides absolute control over the document, but for casual homework assignments, rigorous control isn't typically needed. Instead, I noticed myself and others searching for a text editor with a rapid and intuitive way of typing math directly into the document. After failing to find such an editor, I created JaTeX.

A productivity & organization tool. Glance was originally intended for my use only, and is therefore not user-friendly.
